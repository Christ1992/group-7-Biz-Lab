Why WearCast?

People have the daily problem to decide what to wear. Unsuitable clothes will ruin your day for being too cold or too hot. So with WearCast, you could find daily outfit inspirations generated according to weather condition.

Who are we?

WE ARE a student entrepreneur team from KTH, Sweden. We try to discovere the usage of open data and the potential value behind it. WearCast is the final project built for Business Development Lab course which give us the first entrepreneurship experience.
